# WebPrivate - Control Your Online Experience

Take control of your online activities with **WebPrivate**, the ultimate website blocker for Linux. Designed to help you stay focused and productive, WebPrivate allows you to block distracting websites, manage your browsing time, and create a healthier online environment.

## Why Choose WebPrivate?

- **Enhance Productivity**: Eliminate distractions and increase your productivity by blocking time-wasting websites.
- **Focus on What Matters**: Spend more time on meaningful activities and less on mindless browsing.
- **Support Your Goals**: Whether you're working, studying, or simply trying to reduce screen time, WebPrivate helps you achieve your goals.
- **Open Source**: WebPrivate is an open-source project, ensuring transparency and community support.

## Features

- **Website Blocking**: Block distracting or time-wasting websites easily and effectively.
- **Customizable Blocklists**: Create personalized lists of websites you want to block, or use pre-configured ones.
- **Productivity Insights**: Track your browsing habits and monitor how much time you spend on blocked websites.
- **Flexible Control**: Set time limits, block specific categories of sites, or enforce daily limits to stay on track.
- **Cross-Browser Support**: Works with major browsers to give you complete control over your online experience.
- **Lightweight and Efficient**: Designed to run smoothly on Linux systems without using unnecessary resources.

## Usage

1. Open the WebPrivate app in your browser.
2. Configure your website blocklists by adding specific URLs or selecting categories of websites to block.
3. Set time limits for your browsing sessions or restrict certain sites at specific times.
4. Monitor your progress and adjust your settings as needed to stay focused and productive.
