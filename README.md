# 🛡️ WebPrivate – Control Your Online Experience

Take control of your digital habits with **WebPrivate**, the ultimate **website blocker for Linux**. Whether you're working, studying, or just trying to reduce distractions, WebPrivate empowers you to block unwanted content and build a healthier online routine.

---

## 💡 Why Choose WebPrivate?

- ⚡ **Boost Your Productivity** — Eliminate digital distractions and stay focused on what matters  
- 🎯 **Stay on Track** — Reduce mindless browsing and align your screen time with your goals  
- 👁️ **Full Transparency** — 100% open source and community-driven  
- 🧠 **Mindful Browsing** — Create a balanced relationship with your digital world

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔒 **Website Blocking** – Block distracting or time-wasting websites with ease  
- 📝 **Custom Blocklists** – Create your own lists or use pre-built categories  
- 🌐 **Cross-Browser Support** – Compatible with all major browsers  
- 🐧 **Optimized for Linux** – Lightweight and efficient, designed specifically for Linux systems

---

## 🧭 How to Use

1. 🚀 Launch **WebPrivate** in your browser  
2. 🔧 Configure your **blocklists** (by URL or by category)  
3. ✅ Enjoy a more focused, productive browsing experience!
